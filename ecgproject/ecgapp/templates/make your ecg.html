Make Your Own ECG System

ECG System Circuit Diagram

This circuit diagram shows how to connect:

  • AD8232 OUT (Analog) to Arduino Uno A0 pin
  • AD8232 GND to Arduino GND
  • AD8232 3.3V (or 3V) to Arduino 3.3V pin
  • LO+ and LO- can be left unconnected or monitored via digital pins
  • Electrodes: RA (Right Arm), LA (Left Arm), RL (Right Leg/Ground)
  • Upload Arduino code to read analog signal from pin A0 and print via Serial Monitor
